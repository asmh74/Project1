Play Crystal Collector!

How to play


  1. Once the game starts, you'll be given a randomly generated number
  2. Click a crystal and add points to your score!
  3. If your score matches the random number, you win!
  4. If you guess wrong, you lose!.
  5. Values of crystals are hidden until clicked.
  6. Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each crystal.
  7. Below, there are four different crystals.
    • Red
    • Green
    • Blue
    • Yellow
